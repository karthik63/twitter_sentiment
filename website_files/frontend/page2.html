Spatial Analysis

As expected, The majority of tweets about easter are positive and most of originate from countries with a Christian majority like the USA, Countries in Northern Europe, South Africa and Australia.

A very similar spatial distribution is observed with the keyword "jesus"

The majority of the tweets about Trump are negative and originate from the USA.

Most tweets about BTS originate from South - East Asia, which is to be expected as BTS is a Korean pop band.

Tweets about India are mainly clustured around India.
